Introduction

Health And Rural Development (HARD) Balochistan is a non-profit, non-political, non-religious and non-governmental organization of Pakistan. The Organization is registered in Social Welfare Act 1961 Later in 2014 registered in Society Act 1860 under the law of Pakistan. The organization was established by a group of young development professionals who were motivated while working development sector to serve the people of Balochistan. Initially, the young group members started working from one room office at community level in District Khuzdar. At first step they planned to organize communities and engage them in development and social welfare related voluntary activities on self-support basis. Spending years on voluntary work as from 1996 to 2002 the group members developed trust among communities and decided to formally register the organization naming “Health And Rural Development (HARD) Balochistan”.

Vission

A gender responsive society without poverty, injustice where everyone has right and decent place to live freely on the Earth.

Mission

To advocate for the rights of people especially women and marginalized groups for alleviating poverty and striving to create a culture of justice, equality, peace, democratic governance and environmental protection.

Objectives

To create peaceful and inclusive societies and protect Human rights and human rights defenders to reduce religion, sect ethnic and tribe based conflicts and promoting tolerant and positive behaviors among the people.

Core Values

Humanity, Equity And Justice, Mutual Respect, Accountability And Transparency, Humility And Courage of Conviction, Solidarity with Poor and Helpless.

Thematic Areas (Programs)

Human Rights, Women Rights & Gender Equity, To end Poverty & Injustice, Health & Nutrition, Education & Literacy, Democracy & Governance, WASH, Transgender, Child, Youth & Women Empowerment, Community Development & Capacity Building

People we work for: (Our Focus)

HARD mostly focus the diverse categories of people and groups including the poorest and most excluded, men, women, children and youth in Pakistan. HARD directly works with the communities and community based organization, respective district line departments and other community women and youth groups at local level and such relationships with communities tend to be long term and intensive.

Our Approach

HARD strives to achieve its vision with the support of its partners, communities and other stakeholder’s by embedding HRBA’s (Human Rights Based Approach) three broad and interconnected components; empowerment, solidarity and campaigning and alternatively need based service delivery and provision of basic needs.

Organizational Structure

HARD Balochistan has a standard organizational structure which relies on the democratic values and professional attitude in order to sustain the organizational operations for serving the people. It has General body (common voting members) which is based on 15 members. These GB members democratically elect the seven members of Governing Body.

Institutional Capacity of organization

As an institution, HARD has earned sufficient capacity to initiate innovative and exclusive activities/interventions and manage them successfully to contribute for the great cause of protecting rights and helping the poor and excluded people of the Balochistan province.
